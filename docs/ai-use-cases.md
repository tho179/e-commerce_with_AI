# AI Use Cases In E-commerce (Survey)

## 1) Discovery and Conversion

- Search ranking and semantic retrieval: understand intent beyond exact keyword.
- Personalized recommendation: next-best-product, cross-sell, bundle suggestions.
- Dynamic merchandising: reorder listing by predicted conversion and margin.

## 2) Trust and Product Understanding

- Review intelligence: sentiment, aspect extraction, risk alerts.
- Fake review detection: detect abnormal writing patterns and burst activity.
- Product attribute enrichment: auto-tag specs/colors/sizes from text and images.

## 3) Pricing and Promotion

- Price elasticity prediction by segment and category.
- Promotion targeting: choose customer x product x time window.
- Markdown optimization for slow-moving inventory.

## 4) Operations and Fulfillment

- ETA prediction and shipping risk early warning.
- Return risk scoring to reduce fraud and optimize reverse logistics.
- Inventory demand forecasting for replenishment.

## 5) Customer Service and Retention

- Support ticket triage and intent routing.
- Conversational assistant for order status/refund/product Q&A.
- Churn propensity and win-back campaign optimization.

## 6) Compliance and Safety

- Fraud detection in payment/checkouts.
- Content moderation for review/comment abuse.
- Explainability and audit trails for automated decisions.

## Prioritization Matrix For This Repo

- High impact + low implementation friction: Review Intelligence (implemented).
- High impact + medium complexity: Recommendation hybrid with review sentiment (implemented).
- Medium impact + medium complexity: Demand forecast and dynamic pricing (planned).
- High impact + high complexity: Fraud graph and real-time anomaly detection (planned).
